# Character Sheet — Locked Slide Copy

Single source of truth for every word that appears on the 10 LinkedIn carousel slides. Proofread before pasting any of this into Claude Design — fixing typos there burns weekly quota.

Convention used below:
- `Title` is the big banner word.
- `Rarity line` is the small line under the banner. The rarity word (LEGENDARY / EPIC / RARE / UNCOMMON) is colored per the key in `CLAUDE-DESIGN-PROMPTS.md`.
- `Quote` is the italic line in quote marks.
- `Stats` lines: amber values, brown labels.
- `Favorites` (optional): same styling as Stats.
- `Description` MUST start with `—` (em-dash) and is NOT wrapped in quote marks.
